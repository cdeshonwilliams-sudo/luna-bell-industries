About Luna-Bell Industries

Bay Area's Trusted Environmental Inspection Experts

Luna-Bell Industries specializes in detecting environmental hazards that can threaten the health and safety of homes, commercial properties, and worksites. Led by Project Manager Benjamin Fortune, our team brings industry-leading expertise, rigorous protocols, and certified laboratory analysis to every inspection.

Serving the Greater Bay Area

Our Mission

To provide accurate, timely, and reliable environmental testing that empowers property owners, buyers, and tenants to make informed decisions.

Why It Matters

Undetected asbestos, lead, mold, and radon can cause serious long-term health risks. Early detection is critical to protecting your family and investment.

Our Commitment

Every report we deliver meets or exceeds regulatory standards — backed by certified lab analysis and clear, actionable findings.

What We Test For

Comprehensive Environmental Testing Services

🏗️

Asbestos Testing

Bulk sampling, air monitoring, and full asbestos surveys for residential and commercial properties. Required before renovations or demolitions on older structures.

EPA Method
🔬

Lead Sampling

Surface wipe sampling, soil sampling, and paint chip analysis to detect lead contamination — critical for homes built before 1978.

XRF & Lab Analysis
🏠

Lead Hazard Evaluation

Full risk assessments and hazard evaluations that identify the extent of lead hazards and provide recommended abatement options.

HUD Compliant
🌿

Mold & Radon Inspection

Visual inspections, air sampling, and swab testing for mold growth, combined with radon gas measurement to assess indoor air quality risks.

Air Quality Testing
🧪

PCT Testing

Post-construction and post-remediation clearance testing to confirm environmental hazards have been successfully removed or mitigated.

Clearance Certified
